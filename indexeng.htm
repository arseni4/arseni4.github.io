WHAT IS AQUABLASTING?
Delicate cleaning of metals, plastic, rubber from oxidation, rust, carbon deposits. Aquablasting, or hydroblasting, allows you to gently clean virtually any surface without damage. High pressure water jet cleaning with mild abrasive material removes stubborn dirt, carbon deposits, oil, resins and paint from the surface. Curiously, unlike dry abrasive cleaning methods, the material is not damaged. Aquablasting is used in the cases, when treated surfaces are to be cleaned delicately and thoroughly.

WHEN YOU MIGHT NEED AQUABLAST.LV

AUTOMOTIVE VEHICLES
Repairing auto parts and engines requires careful and gentle cleaning. Aquablasting removes everything, even hard-to-reach dirt from any surface. It does not harm the geometry of the part, therefore it is able to clean those parts that cannot be sandblasted.
RESTORATION WORKS
In restoration work when removing rust, it is very important not to harm the material. We choose the right abrasive for every part so that there are no consequences from cleaning.
